About AWSIS

Advancing Water Security through Open Data and Community Empowerment

{# Mission Section #}
Our Mission

Empowering Communities with Water Data

The Alliance for Water Security in the Indian Sundarbans (AWSIS) is an open data platform designed to transform how communities, researchers, and decision-makers access and utilize water-related information in the Sundarbans region.

By centralizing critical data on water quality, climate patterns, and infrastructure, we enable evidence-based decision making that directly impacts the lives of millions who depend on sustainable water resources.

"Water is the driving force of all nature. Through open data, we transform this force into knowledge that empowers communities."
{# Vision Section #}
Our Vision

A Water-Secure Future for All

We envision a world where every community has access to reliable water data, enabling them to protect their water resources, adapt to climate change, and build resilient livelihoods for generations to come.

{# Objectives Section #}

Our Objectives

Key pillars guiding our mission to advance water security

Data Centralization

Consolidate water-related datasets from multiple sources into a single, accessible platform for streamlined research and analysis.

Evidence-Based Decisions

Provide actionable insights and visualizations that support informed policy-making and resource allocation decisions.

Community Empowerment

Enable local communities to participate in data collection and benefit from water security insights that affect their daily lives.

Technical Capacity

Build local technical capacity for data management, analysis, and the sustainable operation of water information systems.

{# Focus Areas Section #}

The Sundarbans Focus

Understanding the unique water challenges of the world's largest mangrove forest

Salinity Intrusion

Monitoring saltwater encroachment into freshwater sources affecting agriculture and drinking water.

Climate Vulnerability

Tracking cyclone impacts, sea-level rise, and changing monsoon patterns on water availability.

Arsenic Contamination

Mapping groundwater arsenic levels to protect communities from chronic exposure risks.
